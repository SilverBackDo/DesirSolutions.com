# Master Services Agreement Template

This template is the public trust-package summary version of the Desir Solutions master agreement.

Operating posture:

- legal entity: `Desir Solutions LLC`
- governing law: `Washington`
- venue: `King County, Washington`

## Core Terms

1. Services are defined only through an executed SOW.
2. Out-of-scope work requires written approval.
3. Default payment terms are `Net 15`.
4. Late undisputed invoices may incur `1.5%` monthly interest and may trigger work suspension after written notice.
5. Client owns bespoke deliverables after full payment.
6. Desir Solutions retains background IP, templates, methods, and reusable materials.
7. Confidential information is protected for `5 years`, except trade secrets.
8. Services are performed on a professional and workmanlike basis.
9. Liability is capped at fees paid or payable under the affected SOW during the previous `12 months`, subject to customary exclusions.
10. Either party may terminate for uncured material breach after written notice.

Use the client-facing contract in the private operating repository for the full signature-ready document.
