# DBA Decision Memo

## Purpose

Decide whether `Desir Solutions` should be the filed legal entity name or a DBA attached to another legal entity name.

## Current Branding Reality

- Public brand: `Desir Solutions`
- Prior draft legal name convention: `Desir Consultant LLC dba Desir Solutions`

That split is workable, but it creates more admin work than a brand-aligned legal name.

## Archived South Dakota Rule

South Dakota requires a fictitious-name filing for a business operating under an assumed name unless:

- the business name plainly shows the true surname of each interested person, or
- the business name is already on file with the Secretary of State in a required business filing

That means:

- if the filed legal name is `Desir Solutions LLC`, a separate DBA for `Desir Solutions` is generally not needed
- if the filed legal name is `Desir Consultant LLC` and the public brand remains `Desir Solutions`, the DBA should be filed

## Options

### Option A: File the Legal Entity as `Desir Solutions LLC`

Pros:

- public brand and legal name match
- no separate DBA administration for the main brand
- cleaner contracts, invoices, banking, and procurement review
- lower risk of name confusion

Cons:

- depends on availability of the exact or acceptable brand-aligned legal name
- may require updating internal references that still say `Desir Consultant LLC`

### Option B: File `Desir Consultant LLC` and Register `Desir Solutions` as a DBA

Pros:

- preserves the historic internal naming pattern already present in some drafts
- lets the public brand stay `Desir Solutions`

Cons:

- requires separate DBA filing, renewal, and amendment handling
- adds more contract and invoicing complexity
- increases the chance of mismatch between legal name, bank account, and customer paperwork

## Recommendation

Recommended default:

- file a brand-aligned legal entity such as `Desir Solutions LLC` if the name is available

Fallback if that name is unavailable or strategically unwanted:

- file `Desir Consultant LLC`
- file `Desir Solutions` as the DBA immediately after or at the same time the legal entity is formed
