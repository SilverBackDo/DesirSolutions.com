# Archived South Dakota Formation Packet

Archived research only. Desir Solutions is currently aligned to a Washington operating posture, so this packet should not be used as current filing guidance.

It is based on South Dakota Secretary of State and South Dakota Department of Revenue guidance. It does not replace legal or tax advice.

## Filing Path

Use the domestic South Dakota LLC path if the entity will be formed in South Dakota.

Use the foreign-registration path instead if the entity is being formed in another state first and only qualified in South Dakota afterward.

## Exact Domestic LLC Checklist

1. Search business name availability for the exact legal name you want to file.
2. Decide whether you are filing as:
   - `Desir Solutions LLC` or similar brand-aligned legal name
   - `Desir Consultant LLC` with a separate DBA for `Desir Solutions`
3. Reserve the LLC name only if you will not file immediately.
4. Decide filing method:
   - online filing: immediate processing and immediate PDF certificate download
   - paper filing: slower processing and additional paper fee
5. Confirm the South Dakota registered agent before starting the filing.
6. Prepare the Articles of Organization fields:
   - Article I: exact legal name with LLC designator
   - Article IA: purpose, if you want to include one
   - Article II: initial designated office street and mailing address
   - Article III: South Dakota registered agent name and either SD address or commercial agent CRA number
   - Article IV: organizer names and addresses
   - Article V: duration if not perpetual
   - Article VI: member-managed or manager-managed election
   - Article VII: beneficial owners if you choose to include them
   - Article VIII: any additional provisions
   - organizer signatures and titles
7. Save the filing receipt and formation certificate immediately after submission.
8. Create the internal evidence folder with:
   - filing receipt
   - stamped Articles of Organization or certificate
   - registered-agent agreement
   - screenshot or PDF of the entity search result
9. Apply the post-filing contract worksheet in `docs/archive/south-dakota/POST_FILING_CONTRACT_COMPLETION.md`.
10. Update the public website and trust materials with final filed details if the legal name or notice path changed.

## South Dakota Filing Benchmarks

- Domestic LLC Articles of Organization: `$150` online or `$165` by paper.
- Annual report: `$55` online or `$70` by paper.
- Name reservation: `$25`.
- Registered-agent change statement: `$10`.
- Paper filing fee for online-capable filings: additional `$15`.

## South Dakota Registered Agent Requirement

The registered agent must be a South Dakota resident or qualifying business with both:

- a physical address in South Dakota
- a mailing address in South Dakota

If you use a commercial registered agent, capture the CRA number in the filing packet.

## Annual Report Control

- Annual reports are due every year on the first day of the anniversary month of filing.
- Filing opens two months before the due date.
- The late fee starts two months after the due date.
- The operator calendar should contain both the filing-open date and the delinquency date.

## Post-Filing Business Setup

1. Obtain or confirm the FEIN from the IRS.
2. Confirm South Dakota Department of Revenue registration and whether any service, sales, or use tax obligations apply to the actual services sold and where they are received.
3. Open the bank account in the exact filed legal name.
4. Update contracts, invoice templates, and remittance details to match the filed entity.
5. Confirm insurance, accounting, and payment processor profiles use the exact legal name.

## If Washington Operations Continue

If the business still operates from Washington, hires there, or holds itself out as doing business there, review Washington foreign-registration and business-license requirements separately. South Dakota formation does not by itself answer Washington nexus or licensing questions.
