## Release Gate

Do not send customer-facing legal documents until all of the following are true:

1. filed legal name is final
2. DBA decision is final
3. notice address is final
4. signatory authority is final
5. tax and remittance setup matches the legal name
6. counsel or commercial owner has approved the packet
