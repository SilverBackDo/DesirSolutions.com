## Launch Goal

Prepare Desir Solutions for launch with:

- a South Dakota legal entity posture
- public website and trust language aligned to that posture
- contract drafts that can be completed with the filed legal name and notice details
- finance and tax setup that no longer assumes Washington-specific obligations

## Required Before Open Launch

1. Confirm name availability with the South Dakota Secretary of State for the exact legal entity name to be filed.
2. File the LLC or other chosen entity in South Dakota and save the filing receipt and formation documents.
3. Appoint a South Dakota registered agent with both a physical and mailing address in South Dakota.
4. Confirm whether `Desir Solutions` requires a South Dakota fictitious-name or DBA filing based on the final filed legal name.
5. Obtain or confirm FEIN, bank-account setup, and accounting-system legal-entity settings using the filed company name.
6. Set the annual report due month and put recurring filing reminders into the operator calendar.
7. Confirm South Dakota Department of Revenue registration and whether any state or municipal sales/use-tax obligations apply to the actual services sold.
8. If the business still operates from Washington or hires there, confirm whether Washington foreign registration, local licensing, payroll, or tax obligations also apply.
9. Replace placeholders in the MSA, SOW, DPA, invoice templates, and notice contacts with the filed legal details before signature.
10. Re-check the public website footer, contact page, and trust center after filing so they match the final entity posture.
