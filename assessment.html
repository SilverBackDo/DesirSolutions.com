What the Assessment Covers

This offer is designed for buyers who know something is wrong or delayed in the environment but need a senior technical view before committing to a larger project.

Current-State Review

  • Environment and workflow review
  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Tooling and delivery pattern review
  • Operational risk and dependency mapping

Risk & Opportunity Summary

  • Top technical and operational risks
  • Automation opportunities worth prioritizing
  • Quick wins that do not require a major program
  • Constraints that will block phase two if ignored

Decision Package

  • 30-day action plan
  • Recommended sprint or advisory path
  • Initial scope, timeline, and pricing guidance
  • Executive summary for internal decision-makers

Deliverables

  1. Assessment Summary
    A short written review of the current environment, the main friction points, and the most urgent risks.
  2. Priority Register
    A ranked list of fixes, dependencies, and modernization opportunities that the buyer can review internally.
  3. 30-Day Action Plan
    A practical sequence for what should happen next, including quick wins and decisions that cannot be deferred.
  4. Phase-Two Scope
    A recommended modernization sprint or advisory structure with timeline and pricing guidance.

Good Fit / Bad Fit

Good Fit

Buyers with Linux, VMware, automation, or hybrid-cloud issues who need a defined first step and can provide access to the right technical contacts quickly.

Bad Fit

Buyers looking for free architecture consulting, undefined transformation programs, or commodity help desk work without a clear infrastructure objective.

Conversion Path

The assessment converts into either a modernization sprint or a monthly fractional advisory engagement if phase two makes commercial sense.
