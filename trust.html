Trust Center

This page summarizes how Desir Solutions operates today. Binding commercial, privacy, and security obligations are defined in executed agreements, not in marketing copy.

Request Review Materials Terms & Privacy

Operating Model

Desir Solutions is a founder-led consulting business, not a fully autonomous platform and not a large managed-services provider. Client-facing decisions remain human-owned.

Founder-Led Delivery

Scope control, delivery planning, client communication, and commercial approvals stay under named human ownership.

Named Internal Access

Internal systems use role-based access and limited operator permissions for sales, finance, approvals, and administration.

Documented Engagements

Work is governed through a master agreement, statement of work, and written change handling instead of informal scope expansion.

Hosting and Access Controls

The public website and related internal services are designed for a lean consulting business: simple to operate, secured, and documented well enough to support procurement review.

Hosting Stack

  • Oracle Cloud Infrastructure virtual machine hosting
  • Docker-based runtime with reverse proxy and TLS
  • GitHub-driven deployment workflow

Access Model

  • Public access limited to required website endpoints
  • Administrative access restricted to approved operators
  • SSH access limited through controlled administrative access paths

Recovery Controls

  • Database backup routines and restore-drill support
  • Deployment health checks
  • Operational alerting for failures that require review

Data Handling Summary

The public website is intended for business inquiry handling, not for broad customer-data processing.

What the Site Collects

Name, business email, company, role, environment, timing, and inquiry details submitted through the contact flow.

How the Data Is Used

To review inbound requests, create internal follow-up records, and support sales and delivery qualification under human review.

Privacy Requests

Requests can be directed to privacy@desirsolutions.com. Identity verification may be required.

Commercial Readiness

Desir Solutions uses Washington-governed commercial documents and supports standard buyer review for the current stage of the business.

Available on Request

  • Master Services Agreement
  • Statement of Work template
  • Proposal template
  • Invoice and payment terms

Governing Law

Commercial templates and website terms align to Washington law and King County venue unless a signed agreement states otherwise.
