Best fit for operationally sensitive environments with real infrastructure debt.

Desir Solutions is not trying to be everything to every buyer. The strongest fit is where reliability, modernization pressure, and limited internal bandwidth all show up at the same time.

Industry Focus

These sectors match the founder's background and the kinds of problems the firm can solve fastest.

Regional Mid-Market Businesses

Organizations with 20-500 employees that have real server, VMware, Linux, or cloud complexity but not a large internal platform team.

MSPs and Integrator Overflow

Partner teams that need a senior infrastructure delivery resource for scoped assessments, sprint execution, or difficult modernization work.

Distributed Operational Environments

Multi-site or edge-heavy operations where change needs to be sequenced carefully and documented for local teams to absorb.

Typical Buying Signals

If these conditions are true, the assessment offer is usually the right first engagement.

Aging Linux or VMware Estate

Upgrade debt, inconsistent documentation, and uncertainty about what can be changed safely.

Too Much Manual Infrastructure Work

Provisioning, deployment, or operational checks still depend on tribal knowledge and slow handoffs.

Cloud Pressure Without a Clean Plan

Leadership wants movement, but the team lacks a practical sequence for getting from the current environment to a safer target state.

Internal Team at Capacity

Work is not blocked by intent. It is blocked by senior bandwidth and the absence of a tight delivery plan.

If the environment is messy, start with a defined assessment.

The goal is to give the buyer a prioritized plan they can act on immediately, whether Desir Solutions executes phase two or not.
