Services

Consulting built for delivery, not just planning.

Assess legacy environments, define a modernization sequence, and execute operating system, platform, or hosting transitions with clear rollback and handoff plans.

  • Lifecycle upgrade roadmaps
  • Cloud and hybrid migration planning
  • Platform consolidation and remediation

Implementation leadership

Run complex technical programs with milestone ownership, dependency management, and stakeholder reporting that keeps engineering and business decision-makers aligned.

  • Discovery and scoping
  • Executive and vendor coordination
  • Operational transition planning

Automation and IaC

Build repeatable provisioning and deployment patterns that reduce manual steps and configuration drift while making environments easier to audit.

  • Ansible playbooks
  • GitHub Actions and release automation

Solution architecture

Create diagrams, integration workflows, decision records, and operational runbooks that help teams move faster with less ambiguity.

  • Interface and process workflows
  • Runbooks and handoff materials

Delivery hardening

  • Service readiness reviews
  • Post-launch stabilization

Engineering talent placement

  • Role calibration
  • Candidate screening
  • Performance oversight and delivery alignment
