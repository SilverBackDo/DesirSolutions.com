Core Services

These three offers are enough to win the first customers, deliver well, and expand later without selling a service stack the business does not need yet.

1. Infrastructure Stability & Automation Assessment

Price: $4,500 fixed fee
Timeline: 10 business days

  • Stakeholder interviews and current-state review
  • Operational and security risk summary
  • Automation and modernization opportunities
  • 30-day action plan and follow-on scope

2. Modernization Sprint

Price: $12,000-$25,000
Timeline: 2-6 weeks

  • One defined technical outcome, not an open-ended backlog
  • Terraform, Ansible, Linux, VMware, CI/CD, or OCI-related execution
  • Runbooks, handoff notes, and weekly status reporting
  • Controlled change process if scope expands

3. Fractional Infrastructure Lead

Price: $3,000-$6,000 per month
Cadence: Weekly

  • Architecture review and technical decision support
  • Delivery oversight for internal teams or vendors
  • Priority planning, risk review, and escalation support
  • Best fit after an assessment or sprint has already defined the work

Best-Fit Problems

Desir Solutions is strongest when the buyer has an infrastructure problem that is expensive to ignore but too specific for a general IT provider.

Linux / RHEL Modernization

Lifecycle upgrades, hardening cleanup, runbook creation, and operational risk reduction in environments that have drifted for too long.

VMware and On-Prem Cleanup

Dependency mapping, modernization sequencing, environment rationalization, and better change planning before migration or consolidation work begins.

Infrastructure Automation

Terraform and Ansible patterns, GitHub Actions deployment flows, and repeatable operating procedures that reduce manual build and deployment risk.

Hybrid Cloud Planning

Practical planning for OCI, Azure, or mixed environments where cloud adoption has to align with operational reality, not just architecture diagrams.
